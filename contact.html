Common questions

We're here to help you plan your perfect journey. Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about working with Travel enjoyed.

What are typical timelines for trip planning?

Most custom itineraries are delivered within 5-7 business days after our initial consultation. For complex multi-destination trips or peak travel seasons, we recommend reaching out 2-3 months in advance to ensure availability and the best rates.

How do I get started with Travel enjoyed?

Simply reach out through our contact form, email, or phone. We'll schedule a brief consultation to understand your travel style, preferences, and budget. From there, we'll craft a personalized itinerary that matches your vision perfectly.

What information do you need from me?

We'll ask about your travel dates, preferred destinations, budget range, accommodation preferences, and any special interests or activities you'd like to include. The more details you share, the better we can tailor your experience.

How does communication work during planning?

We stay in close contact throughout the planning process via your preferred method—email, phone, or messaging. You'll receive regular updates, and we're always available to answer questions or make adjustments to your itinerary.

What happens after my itinerary is delivered?

You'll receive a comprehensive travel guide with all bookings, confirmations, and recommendations. We provide 24/7 support during your trip for any changes or assistance needed. After you return, we'd love to hear about your experience.
